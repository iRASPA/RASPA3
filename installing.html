Installing

Download one of the precompiled packages from

‍https://github.com/iRASPA/RASPA3/releases

In Figure one can find the list of packages. These include packages for macOS (both intel and apple silicon), windows (both intel en arm64) and many Linux distributions.

![List of available binary packages: Linux distributions based on Red Hat and OpenSUSE (rpm-packages), Debian (deb-packages), and Arch Linux (tar.zst packages), installers for intel- and apple-silicon macs, and installers for intel- and arm64-Windows computers.](introduction/raspa3_releases.png){#fig: binary_packages width="95%"}
